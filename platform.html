Clinical Workflow

1

Patient Enrollment

Oncologist inputs baseline clinical data, uploads genomic report, and enters tumor characteristics. System generates initial predictions across all treatment options within 2 seconds.

2

Treatment Initiation

Use therapy rehearsal to compare strategies. System shows predicted outcomes, toxicity profiles, and molecular rationale. Start recommended therapy with confidence.

3

Continuous Monitoring (Weeks 0-12)

Upload imaging, ctDNA, and biomarkers every 3 weeks. Digital twin updates predictions in real-time. Alerts flag suboptimal response or emerging resistance.

4

Early Decision Support (Week 6)

System predicts final pathologic response with 92% accuracy. Recommend therapy adaptation if needed. Prevent treatment failures before surgery.

5

Post-Treatment Surveillance

Monitor ctDNA monthly for molecular relapse. Detect recurrence 6-12 months before imaging. Enable early intervention and salvage therapy.
