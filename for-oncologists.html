Clinical Use Cases

🎯 Treatment Selection

Scenario: Newly diagnosed 14-year-old with osteosarcoma

How Digital Twin Helps:

  • Input genomics, tumor size, location
  • Get predictions for MAP, Everolimus, clinical trials
  • See molecular biomarkers supporting each option
  • Choose optimal first-line therapy

Outcome: Precision therapy selection based on patient's unique biology

⚠️ Early Response Assessment

Scenario: Week 6 of MAP chemotherapy, uncertain response

How Digital Twin Helps:

  • Upload week 6 imaging + ctDNA + labs
  • System predicts final necrosis: 65% (borderline)
  • Alert: "Consider therapy intensification"
  • Therapy rehearsal shows better outcome with addition of targeted agent

Outcome: Early adaptation prevents treatment failure

🔬 Resistance Monitoring

Scenario: Week 9, patient not improving

How Digital Twin Helps:

  • Serial ctDNA shows new KRAS mutation
  • Alert: "Resistance mutation detected"
  • Recommendation: "Add MEK inhibitor"
  • Prevents waiting until week 12 failure

Outcome: Salvage therapy started 6 weeks earlier

🚨 Relapse Detection

Scenario: 8 months post-treatment, routine surveillance

How Digital Twin Helps:

  • Monthly ctDNA monitoring shows detectable level
  • Alert: "Molecular relapse detected"
  • Imaging still clear
  • Early restaging reveals microscopic disease

Outcome: Treatment initiated 6 months before radiographic relapse
