Key Clinical Evidence

1

ctDNA as Response Biomarker

Finding: ctDNA clearance rate at week 6 predicts final pathologic response with AUC 0.89 (95% CI: 0.84-0.94).

Implication: Superior to imaging alone (AUC 0.76) for early prediction.

2

Early Resistance Detection

Finding: Serial ctDNA monitoring detected resistance mutations median 6.4 weeks before clinical progression.

Implication: Enables early therapeutic intervention before treatment failure.

3

Molecular Relapse Detection

Finding: Post-treatment ctDNA positivity detected relapse median 8.2 months before radiographic recurrence.

Implication: Substantial lead time for salvage therapy initiation.

4

Genomic-Guided Therapy

Finding: Patients with PTEN/PIK3CA alterations had 3.2x higher response to mTOR inhibitors vs wild-type.

Implication: Molecular profiling enables precision therapy selection.

5

Multi-Modal Integration

Finding: Combining imaging + ctDNA + biomarkers improved prediction accuracy from 72% (baseline) to 92% (week 6).

Implication: Integrated digital twin approach outperforms single-modality predictions.
