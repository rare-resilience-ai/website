Therapeutic Areas of Interest

mTOR Inhibitors

Target Population: Patients with PTEN/PIK3CA alterations (~30% of osteosarcoma)

Biomarker: PI3K pathway activation score

Endpoint: ctDNA clearance at 6 weeks

Opportunity: Enriched trial population, accelerated approval pathway

CDK4/6 Inhibitors

Target Population: RB1 wild-type patients (~70%)

Biomarker: RB1 status, cell cycle signature

Endpoint: Pathologic response, ctDNA dynamics

Opportunity: Precision selection, combination strategies

Immunotherapy

Target Population: High immune infiltration, TMB-high

Biomarker: Immune signature, PD-L1, TMB

Endpoint: Durable response, ctDNA clearance

Opportunity: Identify the 15-20% who respond
